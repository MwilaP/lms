## WilaMotors LMS
WilaMotors Learning Management System is a comprehensive training platform designed to support employee development, technical training, and knowledge management across the organization.

### About
WilaMotors LMS is built to deliver a modern, user-friendly learning experience. This platform supports our commitment to continuous learning and professional development across all departments, from technical training for mechanics and engineers to compliance and safety courses for all staff.

### Key Features

- **Structured Learning**: Organize training content with a 3-level hierarchy - courses contain chapters, which contain lessons. Perfect for technical manuals, safety procedures, and compliance training.

- **Live Training Sessions**: Group employees into batches based on department, role, or training program. Schedule live training sessions with integrated video conferencing for real-time instruction.

- **Assessments and Assignments**: Evaluate employee knowledge with quizzes (single-choice, multiple-choice, or open-ended questions) and practical assignments. Track completion and performance across the organization.

- **Professional Certification**: Issue certificates upon course or batch completion. Use built-in templates or create custom certificates that align with WilaMotors branding and industry standards.
